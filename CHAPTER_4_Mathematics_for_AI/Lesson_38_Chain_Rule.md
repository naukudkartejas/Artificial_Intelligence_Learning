# Lesson 38: Chain Rule

> Goal: Understand how derivatives pass through multiple connected
> functions.

Neural networks are layers connected together.

``` text
Input
 │
 ▼
Layer1
 │
 ▼
Layer2
 │
 ▼
Output
```

The chain rule combines derivatives across layers.

Applications: - Backpropagation - Deep Learning

Summary: Without the chain rule, deep neural networks could not be
trained.
