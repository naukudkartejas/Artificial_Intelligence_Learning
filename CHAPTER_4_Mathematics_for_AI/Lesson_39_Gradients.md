# Lesson 39: Gradients

> Goal: Understand gradients and how they guide learning.

A gradient points toward the direction of fastest increase.

AI moves in the **opposite** direction to reduce error.

``` text
Gradient

↑ Increase

↓

Gradient Descent
```

Applications: - Neural Networks - LLM Training

Summary: Gradients tell AI which direction to move.
