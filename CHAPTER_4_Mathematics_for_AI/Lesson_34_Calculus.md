# Lesson 34: Calculus

## Part III -- Mathematics for AI

> Goal: Understand why calculus was invented and why it is essential for
> AI.

### Why was calculus invented?

People needed mathematics to describe **change**. Physics, engineering,
economics, and AI all study systems that change over time.

### Why AI needs calculus

A machine learning model makes mistakes. Calculus tells the model: - How
wrong it is - Which direction reduces the error - How much to update its
parameters

Pipeline:

``` text
Prediction
   │
   ▼
Error
   │
   ▼
Calculus
   │
   ▼
Better Prediction
```

### Applications

-   Neural Networks
-   Deep Learning
-   Optimization
-   Gradient Descent
-   Backpropagation

### Interview

**Q:** Why is calculus important in AI?

**A:** It allows models to optimize parameters by minimizing errors.

### Summary

Calculus is the mathematics of change and optimization.
